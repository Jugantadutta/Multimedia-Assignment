What is Multimedia Images?

Multimedia images are visuals that include various media formats such as photos, drawings, and digital artwork to present information in a more engaging way.

Types of Images:

  • Raster Images: Composed of pixels (e.g., JPEG, PNG, GIF).
  • Vector Images: Composed of paths and lines (e.g., SVG, EPS).
  • 3D Images: Created in three dimensions for realistic renderings.

What is Graphics?

Graphics are visual elements that are used to communicate information or create visual effects in digital or physical media.

Types of Graphics:

  • Bitmap Graphics: Based on pixels (e.g., photographs).
  • Vector Graphics: Made up of paths (e.g., logos, icons).
  • 3D Graphics: Used in animation and games for realistic models.
  • Infographics: Visual representations of data.
